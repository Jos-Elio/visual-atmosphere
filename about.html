About

Music + Sound

Jos Elio Angel works as an independent producer and songwriter based in Leipzig, Germany. He writes, composes, records, produces, mixes and masters music. His compositions are a mixture of recorded analog instruments and sounds like guitar, voice and field recordings, and of digital software instruments like synthesizers, strings, bass, drums and effects. In a broader sense his songs are in the pop genre.

His releases are: soft (album, 2024), and full bloom (single, 2022).

He also works as a sound designer, mixing and sampling sounds and music for artists working in performance, dance, and film.

Visuals + Graphic Design

Visuals: For his own live music shows he creates colorful, ambient visuals consisting of found footage and selfmade video clips. Through varying light intensities and colors, and a visual highlighting of excerpts of the lyrics of the songs, the live shows offer an immersive space.

The Atmosphere Generator is an interactive audiovisual web application created by Jos Elio Angel. It allows users to customize immersive visual experiences through adjustable parameters and presets.

Print + Web Design: Jos Elio Angel also works with printed media and works on commission. For example he designed posters for the Westflügel e.V. (a theater in Leipzig, Germany) and Chiara Mila Jason (Artist & Sexological Bodyworker (in training)).
